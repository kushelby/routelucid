Clutch Cabs

Shared rides. Fixed routes.
Pre-scheduled seats.

A shared mobility marketplace where rides are pre-scheduled with predefined routes, timings, and seat availability. Browse available rides and reserve seats — no surge pricing, no uncertainty.

Smart Route Planning
AI-powered DBSCAN clustering identifies the highest-demand corridors and structures routes along them with mathematical precision.
Pre-Scheduled Rides
Every route has a fixed timetable. Passengers know exactly when the cab arrives at their nearest stop — down to a ±3 minute window.
Seat-Based Booking
Book individual seats, not whole cabs. See who's travelling with you, coordinate as a community, and split costs automatically.
Real-Time Tracking
Live GPS feeds power delay prediction and smart ETA alerts, so passengers always know exactly where their cab is.
Event Commutes
Pre-planned rides to concerts, matches, and festivals from multiple pickup points — return rides included. No surge, no parking chaos.
Affordable & Reliable
Shared seats mean significantly lower cost per passenger than private cabs. Fixed routes mean reliability that builds a daily routine around.
The Intelligence Layer

Routes designed by data,
refined by machine learning

A three-stage algorithm powers every route on the network — from knowing where to operate, to how to structure stops, to continuously improving in the field.

01
DBSCAN Clustering
Demand Identification
We synthesise GHMC ward data, Metro OD surveys, TSRTC boarding counts, CDR signals, and Ola/Uber surge patterns to cluster commuter origins and destinations, ranking corridors by daily trip volume.
02
Google OR-Tools VRP
Route Construction
A Vehicle Routing Problem solver places stops at 500m intervals, assigns precise timestamps with ±3-minute SLAs, and maximises seat fill above 80%. Optimised weekly using real ridership feedback.
03
Generative AI
Live Learning Model
A fine-tuned transformer monitors every active cab in real time — predicting delays, detecting route deviations, and surfacing new corridor recommendations. Every completed trip becomes training data.
